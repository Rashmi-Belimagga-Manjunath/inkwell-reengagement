📊 The Diagnosis

Where the Young Readers Went

Footfall is down 28% across all 9 branches. The drop is steepest among 18–35 year-olds — a segment that now represents only 18% of in-store visits, down from 34% two years ago.

💳

Paper Stamp Cards

—62% younger engagement vs digital loyalty

Under-35s expect a loyalty programme in their pocket, not a creased card. 78% say they'd visit more often with a digital points system they can track on their phone.

📧

No Email List

0 subscribers · zero ability to re-engage

No way to announce events, new arrivals, or Christmas deals. Every independent bookshop with an email list sees 3–5× higher repeat visit rates among subscribers.

📹

No Social Presence

82% of 18–35s discover books via social

Younger readers find their next read on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube — not by walking past a shop window. Inkwell has no discoverable social footprint.

📈 The Opportunity

A €42k Problem with a €5k Fix

Lost Revenue

€42k monthly revenue gap from 28% footfall drop

Across 9 branches, the 28% decline represents ~€42k/month in lost book sales. Recovering just one-third of that within 6 weeks covers the campaign budget 3× over.

Christmas Windfall

+40% typical Q4 uplift for indie bookshops

Christmas is the season bookshops were made for. With a digital loyalty launch timed 6 weeks out, we capture the full holiday shopping curve.

Lifetime Value

3.2× higher LTV for digitally engaged members

A customer who joins the digital loyalty programme and receives emails spends 3.2× more over 12 months than a non-member. The list pays for itself in week one.

📝 The Re-Engagement Plan

Inkwell Rewind — 6 Weeks to Christmas

A phased campaign that builds an email list, launches digital loyalty, and fills every branch through the holiday season.

Week 1

📦 The Hook — "Rewind Your Story" Launch

Install QR-code point-of-sale displays in all 9 branches. Customers scan to join the Inkwell Rewind — a digital loyalty programme that replaces paper stamp cards. First purchase with the card earns double points.

Digital Loyalty Launch In-Store POS
Week 2

📧 The Invitation — Launch Email + Social

First email to everyone who scanned in-store. Content: "You're in. Here's how your Rewind card works." Simultaneous launch on Instagram, TikTok, and the new Inkwell account. Each branch gets its own social takeover day.

Launch Email Social Launch
Week 3–4

🎁 13 Days of Christmas — Advent Campaign

Every day from Dec 1–13, a short email featuring one book recommendation per Inkwell branch, curated by that branch's bookseller. Each email includes a members-only discount code. Social posts mirror each daily pick.

13-Day Drip Daily Social
Week 5

🎒 The Event — Late-Night Christmas Shopping

All 9 branches stay open late (until 10PM) for one night. Members get 20% off, mulled wine, and a free tote bag. Live readings from local authors. User-generated content campaign on Instagram: #InkwellRewind.

Branch Event UGC Campaign
Week 6

🎈 Final Push — "Last-Minute Miracle"

Gift guide email: top 10 books hand-picked by Inkwell booksellers for every type of reader. Free gift-wrapping with Rewind card. Countdown social posts: "5 books left on our shelves, 5 days until Christmas."

Gift Guide Countdown Series
